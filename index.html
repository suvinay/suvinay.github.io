About Me

[More]

I am a Senior Staff Engineer at Google, building AI supercomputing systems (TPUs). I work at the intersection of hardware, systems research, and performance engineering. Previously, I received my Ph.D. from MIT (EECS, CSAIL) and my undergraduate degree at IIT Madras.
